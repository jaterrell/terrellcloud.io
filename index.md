## Cloud & AI systems, designed and delivered

TerrellCloud.io is a professional Cloud & AI Systems consultancy, established
in 2021. We help clients design and implement technology in the cloud and
generative AI space — cloud architecture, AI/GenAI system design, and
hands-on implementation.

We are founder-run and deliberately small. Every engagement is led by a
practitioner who has designed, built, and operated these systems in
production — not handed off to a bench.

### What we do

- **Cloud architecture** — landing zones, migration planning, cost and
  reliability reviews, and architecture designs that hold up under real
  workloads.
- **Generative AI system design** — RAG pipelines, agentic workflows,
  evaluation harnesses, and the guardrails that make GenAI safe to put in
  front of customers.
- **Implementation** — we don't stop at slideware. We build alongside your
  team and leave you with systems you can own and operate.

Read more [about the consultancy](/about/), or head to
[the blog](/blog/) for practitioner notes on cloud and GenAI work.
