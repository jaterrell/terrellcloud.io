TerrellCloud.io is a professional Cloud & AI Systems consultancy,
established in 2021.

We help clients design and implement technology in the cloud and generative
AI space. That covers the full arc of an engagement: understanding the
business problem, designing an architecture that fits it, and implementing
the system — not just recommending one.

## How we work

TerrellCloud.io is founder-run. The person you talk to in the first call is
the person who designs your architecture and reviews the code. That model
keeps engagements small, fast, and honest: we take on work we can do well,
and we say so plainly when something isn't worth building.

Typical engagements include:

- **Cloud architecture and review** — designing new workloads, reviewing
  existing ones against reliability, security, and cost lenses, and
  planning migrations that don't stall halfway.
- **GenAI system design and implementation** — retrieval-augmented
  generation, agentic workflows, model and prompt evaluation, and the
  operational scaffolding (observability, guardrails, cost controls) that
  production GenAI actually requires.
- **Advisory** — a standing practitioner voice for teams making cloud and
  AI decisions without wanting a full-time hire.

## The blog

The [blog](/blog/) is where we write down what we learn in the field —
patterns that worked, anti-patterns we keep meeting, and honest notes on
what cloud and GenAI systems cost to run. It's written for potential
clients and technical peers alike.
